Ways to Lighten Your Foodprint

Food is part of our everyday life and our food choices are crafted by our habits and culture. It is something shared and exchanged. This is why lowering our foodprint often requires shared efforts and collective action of those around us as well. Here is how you can lower your foodprint:

  • 1. Learn more about the impact of food on climate change (like you are doing now!).
  • 2. Spread awareness so others also recognize that food has an impact on climate change.
  • 3. Make conscious decisions on the foods that you buy, eat, share, cater, and promote.
