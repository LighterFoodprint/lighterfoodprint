About

Our mission is to integrate the climate into our everyday food choices.

We help restaurants and food services reduce their climate impact while meeting their strategic goals through a climate labelling system and sustainable business solutions.

The global food system is a major contributor to climate change, responsible for up to a quarter of global greenhouse gas emissions. That is why our food choices matter when it comes to reducing global emissions.

We envision a world where we produce, consume and compost food in a sustainable manner.

What We Do

Climate Labelling

We calculate the climate impact of each menu item and communicate it through our climate label. The labels are designed to be easily understood and easy to integrate into any menu design, with the mission to empower consumers to make more sustainable and informed food purchases.

Sustainability Consulting

We advise our clients on how to lower their environmental footprint while meeting their business objectives using an environmentally, socially and economically sustainable approach.

COVID Recovery

With a focus on building a thriving local economy, we help small and local restaurants meet their strategic goals through COVID. We offer objective, practical and results-driven COVID-recovery advice to struggling food businesses to get them back on their feet in time for a post-COVID world.
